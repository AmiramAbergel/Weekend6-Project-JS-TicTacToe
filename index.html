Tic-Tac-Toe is probably something you've played before.
The game is pretty simple, isn't it? That's what most people think.
However, if you think about it carefully, you'll find that it's not as easy as it seems! Unlike a lot of other games, tic-tac-toe requires you to look ahead and anticipate what the other player might do.
RULES FOR TIC-TAC-TOE
1. The game is played on a grid that's 3 squares by 3 squares.
2. Your friend (or the computer in this case) is X, and you are O.
Players take turns putting their marks in empty squares.
3. Players who get 3 marks (up, down, across, or diagonally) in a row win.
4. The game ends when all 9 squares are filled. A game ends in a tie if no one has three marks in a row.
